### [Use ereport(ERROR), not Assert(), for publisher tuples missing columns.](https://github.com/postgres/postgres/commit/bf7d19be9b18f6f78bd95052a2704259021e6806)
**Noah Misch**

This commit changes error handling for publisher tuples with missing columns from an `Assert()` to `ereport(ERROR)`. This ensures that mismatches between advertised and received column counts, originating from the publisher, are properly handled in production builds, preventing crashes due to malicious or buggy publishers.
